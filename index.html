About Digital Civic Portal

Empowering citizens to participate in urban governance

Our Mission

To create a transparent and efficient platform for citizens to track their resolution, and participate in the governance process through technology.

Key Features

  • Easy issue reporting with photo upload
  • Real-time tracking of issue resolution
  • Transparent tender management system
  • Direct communication with municipal departments
  • Data-driven insights for better governance
